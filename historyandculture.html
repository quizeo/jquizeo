Loboc Man Made Forest: Reforestation Project


As the name suggests, the Man Made Forest was crafted by human hands. A reforestation project
by the local government has brought many students to the site over the course of 30
years. They planted trees every year as part of their curriculum in school!


The reforestation was considered necessary because of the growing “kaingin” industry
(slash-and-burn farming) in Bohol which was threatening the natural environment in the area.
